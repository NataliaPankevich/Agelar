О НАС

AGELAR LLC is a company operating in the Transport, Freight Forwarding&Logistics sector, and committed to providing exceptional services in all these areas.

Our values:

  • Team. We are a group of professionals united by common aims.
  • Client. Producing complex and personalized solution for any requirements based on long-term professional experience to optimize time and costs for each Client.
  • Responsibility. As our Client, you can be sure that we will do everything in our power to fulfil our obligations, and help you if any unexpected trouble comes up.

Our goals:

  • to exceed our customers expectations;
  • to apply the experience of the world's leading transport companies and modern solutions
  • to create ergonomic working conditions;
  • to be useful to each client, to take into account all the wishes and suggestions of our partners and customers;

Our advantages:

  • we ensure safety and timeliness;
  • the possibility of consolidating cargo from several suppliers under one transport document, with the appointment of up to one recipient;
  • we optimise processes and costs;
  • high qualification and responsibility of employees.
