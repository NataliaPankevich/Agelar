Forwarding is a constant and strict control of the movement of cargo from the Supplier (Seller) to the Customer (Buyer).

Our specialists coordinate all changes in the process of moving cargo, based on the calculation of the greatest efficiency. They can also use the scheme of multimodal transportation of our international partners using various modes of transport. At the same time, the Client is always informed about all changes that have occurred in the route, and can find out where his cargo is currently located.

Freight forwarding by company "Agelar" also includes:

  • definition of points for goods reloading and warehousing, as well as calculation of optimal conditions for storage and reloading of goods;
  • determination of the type of loading (side, top, rear) and securing the cargo;
  • preparation of route documentation, its approval;
  • your dedicated vahicle with GPS (acess 24h/day;
  • cargo insurance;
  • controlled loading and unloading;
  • provision of documentary closure of the transportation, which includes customs documents on export / import / transit, confirmation of the recipient.
